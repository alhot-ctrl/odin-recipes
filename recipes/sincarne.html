Chili sin carne

Description

Chili sin carne is a great alternative to Chili con carne

For the best result let the chili cook for about 12 hours

Ingredients

  • 300 ml Gemüsesuppe (klar)
  • 1 Schuss Öl
  • 400 g Tomaten
  • 2 EL Tomatenmark
  • 60 g Sellerie (oder Staudensellerie)
  • 1 Stk Paprika (rot oder grün)
  • 200 g Mais (Dose)
  • 300 g Kidneybohnen (Dose)

Steps

  1. portion the vegetables
  2. cook it
  3. enjoy it
